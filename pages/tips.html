FOLLOW THESE TIPS BEFORE BUYING A NEW BATTERY...

Car batteries, like any other component, have a limited lifespan and must be replaced as soon as issues arise. Having said that, the majority of car batteries on the market are standard; they charge the automobile's essential parts and have a constant 12 V output. But while selecting a battery, a lot of tiny and big factors are taken into account.

Even still, price is still the fundamental criterion for choosing batteries. Car battery prices in Dubai, however, are competitive in a favourable market, therefore this cannot be the only factor taken into account when comparing. Thus, to assist you in navigating the different possibilities available, here are some guidelines to adhere to:

Battery Size

Yes, this is a crucial factor to take into account when selecting an automobile battery. The majority of batteries come in conventional sizes, but before you go shopping for a new one, make sure you measure the height, width, and length of the old battery. Selecting a battery that doesn't fit exactly into your car's battery tray is a bad idea because it could harm the tray and result in fluid spills.

Battery Freshness

A battery manufactured more than a year ago shouldn't be installed. For a year-old battery will undoubtedly have a lower power. Nevertheless, before replacing your automobile batteries, check the code and serial number to determine the manufacturing date. You will know exactly what you are choosing because the serial number is marked with the production year and month.

Battery Capacity must be High.

More capacity means that more power will be accessible for a longer period of time, making it more useful in cases when your car's alternator fails or you need to leave the lights on longer. Higher capacity batteries will not be affected by a drop in charge because they will hold their charge for an extended period of time. Thus, choose for a battery with a larger reserve capacity. Although they could be a little pricey, you will always get something from them.

Maintainance

Each car has a power need, which determines which battery is used. This establishes the battery's storage capacity, which is expressed in Ampere-hour, or Ah. When a battery has a higher Ah, it can withstand a load for a longer amount of time than one with a lower Ah.

Warranty

Yes, this is a crucial factor to take into account when selecting a battery. If there are issues, a battery with a longer warranty is eligible for free replacement or repairs.
